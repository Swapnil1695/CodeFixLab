Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to get a response?

We typically respond within 24-48 hours for standard inquiries. Error reports are prioritized and usually get a response within 12-24 hours. Messages sent on weekends will be answered on Monday morning.

Do you provide help with specific coding problems?

Yes! We help with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript problems. Please provide as much detail as possible: error message, browser/OS information, code snippet, and steps to reproduce the issue. For complex problems, we may suggest checking our Error Fix section or community forum first.

Can I contribute to CodeFixLab?

Absolutely! We welcome contributions from educators and developers. You can submit tutorials, error fixes, or project ideas. Contact us with your proposal, and we'll discuss how you can contribute to our educational mission.

Is CodeFixLab really free?

Yes, our core content is completely free. We believe education should be accessible to everyone. We may offer premium features in the future, but our tutorials, error fixes, and basic source code will always remain free.

Can I use CodeFixLab content in my classroom?

Yes, educators are welcome to use our content in their classrooms. We ask that you credit CodeFixLab as the source. If you need specific materials or have suggestions for educational use, please contact us - we're happy to support educators.

How accurate is the code on CodeFixLab?

All code is tested across multiple browsers and devices before publication. We also update our content regularly to reflect best practices and new web standards. However, web development evolves quickly, so if you find outdated code, please report it to us.
